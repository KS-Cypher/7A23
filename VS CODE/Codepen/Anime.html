NARUTO

Masashi Kishimoto's Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by him. It chronicles the narrative of Naruto Uzumaki, a teenage ninja who aspires to be the Hokage, the village's leader, and wants acclaim from his peers. The series has been licensed in 90 countries, with 35 nations serializing the manga. After striking a distribution contract with Viz Media, Madman Entertainment began publishing Naruto series in Australia and New Zealand in March 2008.
